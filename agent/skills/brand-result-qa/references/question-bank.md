# Brand Result QA Question Bank

Answer all applicable questions unless the user asks for a smaller subset.

## Brand Understanding

1. What is this brand's mission?
2. What is this brand's vision?
3. What are the brand values?
4. What is the brand's main promise?
5. What makes this brand different?

## Audience

6. Who is the primary target audience?
7. What are their main pains?
8. What are their main desires?
9. What objections might they have before buying?

## Voice and Tone

10. Describe the brand's tone of voice.
11. What words or expressions should the brand use?
12. What words or expressions should the brand avoid?
13. Write an Instagram caption in the brand voice.
14. Rewrite this generic paragraph to sound more like the brand: `[insert paragraph]`

## Services

15. What services or products does the brand offer?
16. Which service is best for a new customer?
17. What benefits should be emphasized?

## Website

18. Write a homepage hero section.
19. Suggest a simple sitemap.
20. Write 5 CTA options.

## Sales

21. List common objections and how the brand should respond.
22. Write a short WhatsApp response to a new lead.
23. Write a sales-oriented FAQ.

## Safety and Accuracy

24. What information is missing from the knowledge base?
25. What should the AI assistant avoid saying?
26. What claims require human approval?
